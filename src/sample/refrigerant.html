Refrigerant R-134a
 
Refrigerant Gas (Honeywell R-134a Refrigerant Gas) 
1. High efficiency 
2. High purity 

R-134a Refrigerant Gas Application: Neutral Packing R-134a Refrigerant Gas is an environmental friendly refrigerant with ZERO ODP(ozone-depleting-potential), which is widely used in auto air conditioning, domestic, commercial and industrial refrigeration systems. R-134a Refrigerant Gas can also be applied to commercial refrigeration and industrial refrigeration equipments. R-134a Refrigerant Gas has been long term regarded as the substitute for R12 Refrigerant Gas.
